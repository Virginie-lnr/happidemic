Our Mission

Every day we get so many news from all over the world: about countries, people, animals, plants, technology, environment etc. What most of them have in common? They are really bad news, annoying news, frustrating, negative. They make you feel angry, depressed and sad. Especially in Covid-times everybody needs more positive news. News stories that make you happy again. But it is really difficult to find this news, even on the Internet. Because most magazines and newspapers concentrate on exciting events which are mostly depressing. This is why we created this website: to spread positive news stories. Looking for stories that are or were successful, reading them makes you feel confident. That is what we need now.
